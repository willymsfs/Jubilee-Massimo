Hope Does Not Disappoint — The Catholic Jubilee of Hope 2025

Based on: Dr. Massimo Faggioli, Villanova University Sources: Franciscan Renewal Center Phoenix · Paluch Lecture 2024 (Mundelein) · UCD Dublin 2025
"Hope does not disappoint." The Greek word kataischuno means something deeper: a hope that will never make you ashamed of being hopeful. For Paul, hope is not optimism. It is not a feeling. It is personified by Jesus Christ — and it is the complete opposite of optimism. — Dr. Massimo Faggioli on Romans 5:5 and the Jubilee Bull Spes Non Confundit (May 2024)
